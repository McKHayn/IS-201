Why to Join BYU EMS

Experience

While with BYU EMS, you will gain a lot of valuable experience both in practice scenarios and with real-life patient care! We do a lot of on going trainings to ensure all our EMT's are up to date and well versed in protocals so when the times comes, we are prepared to save a life!

Few Skills You'll Learn:

  1. Pit Crew CPR
  2. Trauma
    • Bleeding Control
    • Shock Treatment
    • long Bone Immobilization
  3. Backboards
  4. Live Births!
  5. Medications
